Meet the Poet

Fart McShmarty spent years of his life
using a bathroom as a thinking space; anything and everything he thinks about
comes straight from the shitter. His wife assumed that when he first met him,
she thought he was 'constipated'. His wedding day was spent not only down the hall,
but down the hall--- to the bathroom. Even now, his family: 3 kids and a dog,
question his bathroom wherabouts. It's resulted in several arguments to the point
where Ms. McShmarty claims he's "nothing without a damn toilet seat"; pitiful.

As a child, he always spent his childhood in the toilet. He often said nothing
in his classes, but whenever he sat in a public toilet, he often impressed his
peers with pyscological statements that enlighten people. Teachers were also inspired,
especially when someone as young is capable of embracing a complex vocabulary.
Farty had a chance to excell and reach several top-performing classes, but the
only thing that held him back was the reason how he was able to deliver such inteligence.

It was almost unbelieveable. It was almost unbelieveable when he managed to find
love interest. In a public unisex bathroom, McFarty delivered very notable opinions
on sandwiches. It wasn't his strongest day in the toilet, but it was just enough to
attract Francine (McShmarty). She overheard his ramlbing while her bladder rumbled peak.
The rest was history. Ironically though, he hasn't changed a bit. You could say that Francine
is still living with the past, whether she likes it or not.
